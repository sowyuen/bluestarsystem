Recruitment Process

1. Job Search
Please submit your resume here.

2. Resume screening
Our selection criteria will base on your qualification, essential skills and work experience. Suitable candidates will be contacted by phone or e-mail for an interview. If there is no suitable vacancy, your details will be entered into Blue Star Human Resource Database.You will then be contacted when a suitable opening is available.

3. Interview
A face-to-face interview will be arranged if you are selected. The interview will consist of a meeting with the manager-in-charge.You can use this opportunity to learn more about the company and the nature of the job. You will be notified of the result by phone or e-mail within 14 working days for a second interview.

4. Acceptance
Selected candidates accepted through the interview process will be notified by phone. A letter of acceptance will also be offered to you officially. Welcome to Blue Star System. Together, we build a better future!
